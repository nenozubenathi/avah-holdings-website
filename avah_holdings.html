What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive financial services designed to keep your business structured, compliant, and growing.

Accounting & Bookkeeping

  • Monthly bookkeeping
  • VAT capturing and submission
  • Management accounts
  • Annual financial statements
  • Monthly and yearly reconciliations
  • Budgeting and cash flow support
  • Bargaining Council submissions

Payroll Services

  • Monthly payroll processing
  • EMP201 / EMP501 submissions
  • Workman's Compensation
  • UIF, PAYE, and SDL compliance
  • Payslip generation
  • Employee onboarding & statutory deductions

Taxation

  • Tax registrations (Income, VAT, PAYE, SDL, etc.)
  • Individual & company income tax returns
  • Provisional tax calculations
  • Tax clearance certificates & directives
  • SARS dispute resolution & appeals
  • Amending SARS registered details

Company Secretarial

  • Company & NPO/NGO registrations
  • CSD & CIDB registration
  • COIDA registration
  • BEE affidavit & beneficial ownership
  • Annual returns & statutory records
  • Tender application and compliance

Business Consulting & Advisory

  • Business growth strategies
  • Financial analysis & forecasting
  • Start-up guidance & structuring
  • Internal controls & risk assessments
  • Compliance advice
  • Restructuring support
